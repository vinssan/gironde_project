INSTRUCTIONS - STAGE 1 (2/10)

The experience takes place in 3 stages, called "stage 1", "stage 2" and stage 3"


THE FIRST STAGE

During the first stage, you will be asked to make choices several times between two lotteries.
The lottery shown on the left side of the screen is called "Lottery A" and the one on the right is called "Lottery B".

For each of these lotteries you will be provided with the following informations:

  1. How much you can potentially get. These elements are called "outcomes".

  2. and the probabilities associated with each of these results.
