INSTRUCTIONS - STAGE 2 (9/10)

What is your task during the stage 2?

The order you set is very important

The first element of your list (when you wrote "1") will be displayed with certainty during stage 3

The second element of your list (when you wrote "2") is less likely to be displayed during stage 3

The third element of your list (when you wrote "3") is even less likely to be displayed during stage 3

The fourth element of your list (when you wrote "4") is even less likely to be displayed during stage 3

Please note that even if the fourth element is less likely to be displayed than the third element, it is still possible that
the fourth element will be displayed and not the third element
The same is effective for the second element.

But remember that the first element of your list will be displayed for sure

Note also that you are not allowed to give the same rank for more than one elements ! it will be to easy !
